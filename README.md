## Quality Assurance and Testing

Quality verification is the foundation of specialized distribution. Every component passes through multiple quality control stages before delivery, ensuring authenticity and reliability.

### Common Quality Procedures:
1. **Visual and microscopic inspection** for markings and physical integrity.  
2. **Electrical and functional testing** against manufacturer specifications.  
3. **Environmental testing** for temperature, humidity, and stress tolerance.  
4. **Compliance certification** under ISO, RoHS, and REACH standards.  
5. **Full traceability documentation** from supplier to shipment.  

Through these methods, customers receive parts that meet strict technical and regulatory standards every time.
